Description

Spicy Tuna Sushi is a flavorful and vibrant dish featuring a delicious blend of tender, raw tuna mixed with a spicy sauce, typically made from mayonnaise, sriracha, and sesame oil. The tuna is finely chopped or cubed, ensuring a melt-in-your-mouth texture. Itâ€™s often paired with a hint of green onions or masago (fish roe) for added texture and umami.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fresh Sliced Tuna
  • Crumbled spicy flakes
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Skin tuna and fillet the meat
  2. Wash then cook the rice till it is sticky
  3. Cut avocado into long slices
  4. Sprinkle spicy crumbs along tuna
  5. Roll together in the rice
  6. Slice the tuna into 6cm pieces
